Custom IgBLAST Database
Project Type: Python

Purpose

While working as a research scientist/engineer at the University of Washington, I commonly received samples from African Green Monkeys (AGM) to study current and potential vaccine candidates for various Coronaviruses. Unfortunately, AGMs are not a common species and the lab expressed the need for studying the AGM genetic sequence in order to fully explore vaccine development.

A walkthrough

I followed as well as reviewed literature that had studied and characterized AGMs in their vaccine / immune studies. From there, I validated the reported sequences in the lab with the help of my colleagues and compiled a genetic sequence dataframe. I loaded the dataframe into a queryable format following the IgBLAST database format. Once loaded, I tested the pre-packaged apps that access the NCBI API to compare the AGM dataframe to existing biological data.
